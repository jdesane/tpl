The Framework

Two Plans. One Brokerage.
Your Choice.

Most brokerages offer one plan and tell you to make it work. LPT Realty was designed differently.

Lane 1 is for agents who want maximum per-deal take-home. Lane 2 is for agents who want to build passive income beyond their own closings.

Both lanes share the same brokerage, same tools, same compliance, same support. What changes is how your money flows.

Maximize Per-Deal Income
Business Builder
100%
You keep your entire GCI. LPT Realty charges a flat $500 per deal, capped at $5,000/year.
  • $500 per core transaction (flat fee, not a percentage)
  • $5,000 annual cap — hit it in 10 deals, then close free
  • $195 processing fee per deal (does not count toward cap)
  • $500 annual fee
  • No HybridShare revenue share on this plan
  • Best for: high-volume agents, solo producers, agents who don't want to recruit
Build Passive Income
Brokerage Partner
80/20
You keep 80% of your GCI. The 20% LPT Realty retains funds the HybridShare revenue pool — and you earn from it.
  • 80/20 commission split per core transaction
  • $15,000 annual cap — once hit, close at full commission
  • $195 processing fee per deal
  • $500 annual fee
  • HybridShare UNLOCKED — 7-tier revenue share from your network
  • Stock grants at 2x multiplier vs Business Builder VERIFY
  • Revenue share income survives retirement VERIFY
  • Best for: agents building a team, agents who want passive income, long-term wealth builders

Decision Framework

Which Lane Is Right for You?

Choose Business Builder If…

  • You close 10+ deals/year and want maximum per-deal income
  • You don't plan to recruit other agents
  • You want the simplest, most predictable fee structure
  • You value keeping every dollar over building passive streams

Choose Brokerage Partner If…

  • You're interested in building a network of agents
  • You want income that doesn't require you to personally close deals
  • You're thinking long-term: retirement income, generational wealth
  • You want stock equity at 2x the standard rate VERIFY
  • You see yourself as a team leader or mentor
Flexibility
