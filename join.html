Common Questions
Frequently Asked
Is there a fee to transfer?

No. LPT Realty charges no transfer or sign-up fee. Your $500 annual fee is deducted from your first closing.

What happens to my active deals?

They transfer with you. LPT's compliance team handles the transition so nothing falls through the cracks.

Do I need to cancel my current brokerage first?

No. Book your call first. We'll walk you through the timing so the transition is seamless.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Once per anniversary year, no penalty. You can move between Business Builder and Brokerage Partner as your business evolves.

What if I'm on a team?

Teams transfer together. Each agent chooses their own plan independently.
