Know Your Fees

Every Fee Your
Brokerage Charges

Most agents don't know their actual cost of doing business. Between splits, caps, desk fees, franchise fees, transaction fees, and tech fees — the total is often far more than you think.

The Breakdown

The 7 Fees Every Agent Should Know

01

Commission Split

The percentage your brokerage keeps from every deal. Ranges from 0% (100% plans) to 50%.

Range: 0% – 50%
02

Annual Cap

The maximum you pay in splits per year. Once you hit it, you close at 100%. Ranges from $5,000 (LPT) to $36,000 (some KW offices).

Range: $5,000 – $36,000
03

Transaction Fee

A flat per-deal processing fee. Typically $50–$400. Charged on every closing.

Range: $50 – $400/deal
04

Desk Fee

Monthly fee for office space/support. $0 at cloud brokerages, $50–$500/mo at brick-and-mortar.

Range: $0 – $500/mo
05

Franchise Fee

Percentage of GCI paid to the parent franchise company. 3%–8% at franchise brands. $0 at independent/cloud brokerages.

Range: 0% – 8% of GCI
06

Annual Fee

Yearly brokerage fee. Ranges from $0 to $1,000+.

Range: $0 – $1,000+
07

Tech / E&O Fee

Technology platform or errors & omissions insurance. Ranges from $0 to $85+/mo.

Range: $0 – $85+/mo
