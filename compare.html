LPT HybridShare

The 7-Tier Revenue Share

Each LPT agent's capped company dollar funds the HybridShare Pool for their transactions. A Brokerage Partner agent who caps contributes $7,500 to the pool (50% of $15K). A Business Builder agent who caps contributes $2,500 to the pool (50% of $5K). An upline sponsor earns their tier percentage of that pool contribution.

Business Builder agents can recruit and build a downline. HybridShare earnings activate once the BB agent upgrades to Brokerage Partner; the downline tree carries over on upgrade, so recruiting activity is never lost.
