People ask me to explain LPT without the marketing. Here it is.

**Splits and cap**

80/20 on every deal up to a $15,000 annual cap to the broker. After cap, you're 100% commission for the rest of the year. Cap resets each anniversary.

**Monthly**

$99/month. That's it. No desk fee, no tech fee, no transaction fee, no franchise royalty, no E&O.

**What you get for that**

Dotloop for transactions. Lofty CRM included. Training library. Broker support. Local compliance. Most agents also plug in a coach or join a team; LPT doesn't require either.

**The two lanes**

LPT has two ways you can participate. Lane one is traditional agent: close deals, hit your cap, keep 100% after. Lane two is revenue share: build a team of agents, earn override on their production, uncapped, vests after 3 years.

You can do one, both, or neither. Nobody forces you into anything.

**The catch**

LPT is a cloud brokerage. No physical office walking distance from you. If you need a desk and a receptionist in your first year, LPT is wrong for you. If you're fine working from a laptop and want the lowest overhead possible while you learn, it fits.
