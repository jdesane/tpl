Hey {{first_name}},

Quick follow-up on the Sponsor Checklist. If you had to rank the twelve questions by weight, the one that predicts the most about your first year is question four:

"What systems will I get access to on day one?"

Most sponsor conversations skip past this or answer it with a vague "we'll get you set up." That answer is how agents end up rebuilding a CRM, writing their own buyer rep, designing their own listing checklist, and generally reinventing an entire business system from scratch while also trying to close deals.

The sponsors who actually have infrastructure will show it to you. Screen share. Logins. A tour of the systems their agents use every day. If that tour doesn't exist, there's nothing for you to plug into.

So the real question underneath question four is: "Can you show me what you've built, right now, on a call?"

Ask it that way. The answer you get will make the decision for you.
