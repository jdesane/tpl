Honest Take

Where Epique Wins

No comparison page is complete without honesty. Here's where Epique still has an edge.

  • Perks package. 70+ agent perks including car washes, professional headshots, roadside assistance, sign installs, and more. No other brokerage bundles this many lifestyle perks. If you genuinely use these services regularly, there's real value — though you're paying for it in the fee structure.
  • Declining cap. Epique's cap drops from $15K to $10K over your first six years. LPT doesn't have a declining cap — but LPT's cap starts at $5K, which is lower than Epique's cap will ever reach.
  • Growth momentum. Epique grew 342% in 2024–2025, from ~300 agents to ~4,000. That's explosive. They've also partnered with Courted for AI-powered recruiting intelligence. They're small but moving fast.
  • Branding and positioning. Epique's "elite" positioning and clean, modern brand attract agents who want a premium feel. Their PowerCON events and global expansion plans signal ambition.
Best Fit

Who LPT Is Best For

  • Agents who want the lowest cost model in the industry — period. If keeping the most money per deal matters more than a perks package, LPT's math is unbeatable.
  • Agents who've calculated what those perks actually cost. $14,348/year in extra fees buys a lot of car washes.
  • Agents who want AI tools that grow their business — not just generate content. Dezzy.ai and RecruitAssist have no equivalent at Epique.
  • Agents who don't want to wait 6 years for a lower cap. LPT's $5K cap is lower than Epique's year-six cap ($10K) — from your first deal.
  • Agents tired of paying $149/month during slow months. At LPT, months with no deals cost you nothing.
