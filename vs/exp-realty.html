Honest Take

Where eXp Wins

No comparison page is complete without honesty. Here's where eXp still has an edge.

  • Scale — 82,000 agents across 24 countries. eXp's referral network is 4x larger than LPT's. If cross-border referrals are a meaningful part of your business, that network has real value.
  • Stock program maturity — EXPI has been publicly traded since 2013. The stock awards at capping, the purchase plan, and the vesting schedule are well-established. LPTA's Nasdaq ticker is reserved but not yet trading — that's a real difference for agents who prioritize equity.
  • Revenue share track record — eXp's rev share program has more years of data behind it. Some agents have built significant passive income through it. LPT's HybridShare is available but younger.
  • Brand awareness — eXp is more widely known in the industry. For agents who rely on brokerage-level marketing or brand recognition in their market, eXp's name carries more weight today.
